About Me

From curiosity to code to conclusions

I'm currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Data Science at The American College, Madurai, building on a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the same institution. Along the way, I've developed a genuine interest in how data — messy, incomplete, and scattered as it often is — can be shaped into something that helps people make better decisions.

During my internship as a Machine Learning Intern at Dot Com Infoway Pvt. Ltd., I got my first real taste of building models that solve actual problems, not just textbook exercises. That experience sharpened my focus: I want to grow into a Data Analyst / Data Scientist role where I can combine statistical thinking, machine learning, and clear communication to help teams act on evidence rather than guesswork.

What keeps me coming back to this field is the problem-solving — the moment a messy dataset turns into a clean pattern, and that pattern turns into an answer someone can actually use.
